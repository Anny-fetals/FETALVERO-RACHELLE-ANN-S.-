It's not just Coffee
It's Starbucks

Starbucks is a global coffeehouse chain known for its high-quality coffee, specialty drinks, and café-style atmosphere. Founded in 1971 in Seattle, Washington, by Jerry Baldwin, Zev Siegl, and Gordon Bowker, it started as a small coffee bean roaster and retailer. The company's transformation into the coffeehouse chain we recognize today is largely credited to Howard Schultz, who joined Starbucks in 1982 and later became its CEO. Schultz is known for popularizing the concept of the "third place" — a comfortable and welcoming space between home and work where customers can enjoy their drinks, use Wi-Fi, or relax.
